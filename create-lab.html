Featured Design Mission

Create a poster for a new STEM invention.

Students design a clear, eye-catching poster for an invention idea. The poster should explain the problem, the solution, the main features, and why the invention would help people.

This task helps students practice design, communication, organization, audience awareness, and STEM explanation without needing advanced tools.

Try This Design Mission

Design Mission Steps

  1. Choose a problem your invention solves.
  2. Name your invention.
  3. Draw or design the main idea.
  4. Label at least three important features.
  5. Explain who it helps and why it matters.
