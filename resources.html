Who can use these?

Resources for learning spaces of all kinds.

CJ STEM Lab resources are designed to be clear, flexible, and useful across classrooms, homes, tutoring sessions, programs, and clubs.

Students

Practice STEM and coding skills with activities that are clear, creative, and manageable.

Families

Use enrichment activities at home to support problem-solving, creativity, coding practice, and project confidence.

Educators

Use templates, rubrics, activity guides, and review sheets to support classroom instruction and project-based learning.

Tutors

Use structured activities for one-on-one support, small groups, review lessons, and enrichment sessions.

Clubs & Programs

Use challenge sheets and creative prompts for STEM clubs, technology clubs, camps, and after-school programs.

Schools

Explore future resource bundles, collaboration options, and materials for STEM events or student enrichment.
