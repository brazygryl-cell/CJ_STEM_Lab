What to Include

Help Ms. CJ understand what you need.

When reaching out, include a short description of what you are looking for so the response can be useful and specific.

For example, you can mention the learner’s grade range, the type of resource you need, whether this is for home or school, and the subject area you want support with.

Send an Email

Helpful Details

  1. Your question or request
  2. Student grade range or age range, if relevant
  3. Topic area: STEM, coding, design, resources, tutoring, or collaboration
  4. Whether you are a family, educator, tutor, school, or program
  5. Any timeline or specific resource need

Email Starters

Not sure what to write?

Use one of these simple starters when contacting CJ STEM Lab.

Family Question

“Hi Ms. CJ, I’m interested in STEM or coding activities for my child. I’d like to know what resources or support may be available.”

Resource Request

“Hi Ms. CJ, I’m looking for a resource related to STEM projects, coding practice, rubrics, or classroom activities.”

Tutoring Inquiry

“Hi Ms. CJ, I’m interested in future tutoring or enrichment support for STEM, coding, or creative technology.”

Collaboration

“Hi Ms. CJ, I’d like to connect about a possible collaboration, school resource, STEM program, or educational project.”
