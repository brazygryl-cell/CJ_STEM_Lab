Today’s Challenge Board

Choose one quick challenge to complete.

These activities are designed to be short, creative, and doable with simple materials or beginner-friendly coding tools.

STEM Challenge

Build a desk organizer for a busy student.

Sketch a desk organizer that holds at least three school supplies. Label each part and explain how your design helps someone stay organized.

  • Draw the design.
  • Label three useful features.
  • Explain the problem it solves.
Coding Warm-Up

Create a button idea.

Imagine a button on a website or app. What should happen when the user clicks it? Write the button label and describe the action.

  • Name the button.
  • Describe what it does.
  • Bonus: write one line of fake code.
Design Mission

Redesign a backpack feature.

Think of one thing students carry often. Design a backpack feature that makes carrying, finding, or protecting that item easier.

  • Choose the item.
  • Sketch the feature.
  • Explain why it helps.
