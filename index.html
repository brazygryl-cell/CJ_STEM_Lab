What is CJ STEM Lab?

A creative tech space for curious learners.

About Ms. CJ

STEM, coding, creativity, and real-world problem-solving.

Her teaching style blends coding, creativity, engineering, design, and problem-solving so students can explore technology in ways that feel meaningful, student-friendly, and connected to the world around them.
