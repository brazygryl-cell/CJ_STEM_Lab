Getting to Villatel — quick travel guide

Most guests fly into Orlando. Below are the easiest options for families and groups.

Nearest airports

  • Orlando International Airport (MCO) — primary commercial airport and usually the fastest drive to International Drive / Villatel area (typical drive ~20–35 min depending on traffic).
  • Orlando Sanford (SFB) — smaller, sometimes cheaper flights but longer drive times for this area.

Shuttle & group transfer options

We’ll organize shared vans for similar arrival times — or you can book a private van for your group.

  • Shared shuttle (budget): Companies such as Mears Connect provide shared & private transfers between MCO and International Drive.
  • Private shuttle/van: Recommended for parties of 6+ — cost splits nicely and delivers you to the villa door.

Car rentals

Renting gives flexibility for groceries, beach trips and local runs. Major brands operate at MCO; book early for vans or multiple SUVs.
